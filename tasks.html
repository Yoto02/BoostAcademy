üìÑ Task 2: Communication, project management and customer centricity

Objective: Combine communication and project management skills to enhance customer experience aligned with strategic priorities.

Mandatory instructions:

  • Each team selects a department represented by at least one of its members.
  • Select a strategic pillar and analyze how these challenges impact or support it.
  • Analyze how communication and project management influence customer satisfaction within the selected department and explain how this supports the chosen pillar.
  • Once the analysis is complete, identify two potential areas for improvement or challenges, and propose a practical improvement initiative based on the concepts covered in class.
