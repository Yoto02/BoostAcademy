Task 1: Strengthening Communication in Customer-Focused Organizations

Objective: Analyze the importance of communication skills and their connection to strategic customer-centric priorities.

Instructions:

  • Select a strategic pillar and relate it to the customer experience.
  • Choose a department represented by one of your team members.
  • Explain how internal communication supports this pillar.
  • Propose improvements that directly impact customer needs.
  • Submit a written document (max 3 pages).
Evaluation Criteria:
  • Content relevance
  • Application of class concepts
  • Team collaboration
  • Writing quality
  • Task 1: Start date: 7/1/025
  • Task 1: Due date: 7/9/2025 4:00 PM
  • You will send the document to the boostacademy email.

Task 3: Conflict Management, Strategic Thinking & Decision-Making

Objective: Use conflict resolution and decision-making concepts in a debate-style team exercise.

Instructions:

  • Teams will be pre-assigned by instructors and will participate in a debate hosted on Microsoft Teams (team vs team).
  • A workplace-related scenario involving challenges such as lack of communication, lack of collaboration, or resistance to change, etc. will be assigned to both teams.
  • One team proposes a solution, the other offers an alternative or complementary approach.
  • Each team will evaluate the other's solution and propose improvements.
  • The session will conclude with a joint reflection or a mutually agreed agreement-building exercise, which both teams will document in writing and post on the Microsoft Teams forum.
Evaluation Criteria:
  • Clarity and coherence
  • Application of course concepts
  • Engagement and respect
  • Decision-making and problem solving
  • Task 1: Start date: 7/24/025
  • Task 1: Due date: 8/1/2025
  • This task will be completed via MS-Teams.
